Proper Posture: Sitting Guide

Step 1: Sit with your back straight and shoulders pulled slightly backward.
Step 2: Keep your feet flat on the floor or on a footrest.
Step 3: Adjust your chair height so your knees are at a 90-degree angle.
Step 4: Place your hips as far back in the chair as possible, aligning your spine with the backrest.
Step 5: Use a cushion or lumbar support for lower back support.
Step 6: Avoid slouching or leaning forward for extended periods.
